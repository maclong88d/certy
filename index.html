What is Certy Network?

Certy Network is a blockchain-based interconnected system advancing the interoperability of Web3 with decentralized services.It offers:

Business and employment-oriented online service

Storage hub for degrees and certificates in NFT-minted form

Portal for recruiters and job seekers

Tokenized learning model to personalize learning pathways suiting career orientation
